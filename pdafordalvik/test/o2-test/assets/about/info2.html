Train journeys that have a paper ticket symbol must be collected from a station ticket machine before you leave.

If we can’t send you a mTicket, we’ll send you a collection reference code. You can find this under ’My Tickets’ in the main menu. You’ll need to type this in to a self-service ticket machine at the station to get your tickets. You’ll also need to have a bank card in your name with you, to put into the machine. But you won’t be charged again. It doesn’t have to be the card that you used to purchase the ticket, but it has to be in your name.

Don’t forget, the collection reference itself isn’t a ticket.

The collection code will stay on your phone for 30 days after the expiry date of your ticket.
