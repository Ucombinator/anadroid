mTickets are available for some journeys; these will show a barcode symbol and will be sent to your phone.

Once we’ve sent you the mTicket, you’ll find it under ’My tickets’ in the main menu. It’ll stay on there for 30 days after the expiry date of your ticket.

At the station, you’ll either need to scan it at the gate, or show it to someone from the train operator to scan.

A mTicket is just like a paper ticket. So when you use it to travel, you might be asked to show it at any time.

You don’t need to be connected to a mobile network or wifi to look at any tickets you’ve bought.

We are unable to provide a receipt for your journey where an mTicket is purchased via the O2 Train Travel Mobile app.
